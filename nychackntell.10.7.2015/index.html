Hardware

  • Custom PCB - Small Batch Assembly - @SmallBatchA
  • 280 LEDs
    • WS2812s (kinda, but some knockoff version)
  • 12v power supply
    • Usable in cars or with 110v AC adapter
  • RFduino rfduino.com

RFduino

  • Arduino compatible chipset w/ built-in BluetoothLE
  • 16Mhz ARM Cortex M0
  • 8Kb RAM/128K Flash
  • only 3.3V!
  • The size of your fingertip

BluetoothLE

  • Not really Bluetooth
  • LE means Low Energy, is actually pretty low energy
  • Used by Fitbit, Apple Watch, etc.

WS2812s

  • 24 bit color
  • high refresh rate
  • Tricky because controlled by timing signal (bitstream) that can't be interrrupted by Bluetooth radio interrupts
  • FastLED to the rescue!

FastLED

  • https://github.com/FastLED/FastLED
  • Formerly FastSPI
  • Active development
  • Supports lots of chipsets & LED types
  • Very responsive project maintainers Daniel Garcia & Mark Kriegsman
    • Responded to our Bluetooth timing issues promptly!
  • Great community

RGBLEDS

  • https://github.com/AaronLiddiment/RGBLEDS
  • Requires/uses FastLED
  • Provides LEDMatrix, LEDSprites, & LEDText libraries
